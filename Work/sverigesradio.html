During one year I was working at Sveriges Radio as a designer, focusing on user experience. Sveriges Radio is Sweden's national publicly funded radio broadcaster.

Visit Sveriges Radio's website: www.sverigesradio.se

WHAT

I was working on the team responsible for the website and also part time on another team working on how to present news in a new way on both the website and on Sveriges Radio's app.

My responsibilities at Sveriges Radio was to make sure the website is accessible and usable for the users and to try out new concepts and features on the website.

HOW

I worked close to developers, UI designers, data analysts and journalists. Also, I had the resposibility to organize user testing and being a part of A/B-tests.

During my time at Sveriges Radio I worked with tools as; Principle, Sketch, Abstract, Invision, Google Analytics, Google Optimize and Trello.
