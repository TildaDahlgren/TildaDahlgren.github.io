Songs get stuck in our head like bubble gum. What way to get rid of that song is better than being given a new sticky song? Bubblelicious is a song generator for you to get a new bubble gum song stuck in your head.

Brief: "Build and launch a product or service using HTML, CSS and Javascript that gets over 1000 unique visitors one week."

WHAT

We were four Hyper Island students during this four week project in October 2016. We were supposed to come up with an idea and create a website around that idea, and we came up with Bubblelicious.

The idea was to create a fun website, which people also would like to visit again. We got our 1000 visit during a week, and do still have visitors coming in.

HOW

The project was divided in to different areas, where I did the UX and UI. I also did the first front end version of our website, in that way presenting the UX and UI.

During the project we were working with HTML, CSS, Javascript, Adobe Photoshop and Adobe Illustrator.
