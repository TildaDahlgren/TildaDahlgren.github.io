Kitten's Cup

DESIGN / CONTENT

A fun drinking game for an upcoming pre-party. Randomized mix of Charades, Doing a Dance Move, Coming up with Captions for Stock Photos amongst other fun activities. Engages both you and your friends.

Brief: "Build and launch a product, working agile."

WHAT

I worked with this product for three weeks at Hyper Island, in a group of six people. While trying to figure out what to build, we did some research of what people like at the moment. Kittens and Beer. Then we decided; we will create a drinking game from scratch, involving kittens and alcohol.

During the project we were supposed to work in an agile environment using a SCRUM board.

HOW

I had the responsibility of the design, where I was working in Adobe Illustrator and Adobe Photoshop. I was also working with the content of the page, creating some of the different drinking games and finding material.

By working in an agile environment and using the SCRUM board we were always working aligned with each other.
