Boomerang International is an old and traditional Scandinavian fashion brand that has been part of the international preppy scene since 1976.

Brief: "How do Boomerang invent and re-launch itself digitally being this traditional brand with a strong heritage while so very fresh and unknown at the same time?"

WHAT

We were five Hyper Island students during this five week project in October-November 2016, analyzing Boomerang's digital presence.

During the project we analyzed the different data collecting tools and social media channels Boomerang are using. With the gained insights we created an analytical data business report including conclusions and suggestions on how Boomerang can improve their customer experience and digital presence.

HOW

We set up three meetings; one kickoff meeting, one mid check-up and the wrap up During the five weeks we divided areas to the different group members, where I took part in the UX, UI and also analyzing their Google Analytics account.

During the project we were working with tools as; Google Analytics, Hotjar and Keywordtool.io.
