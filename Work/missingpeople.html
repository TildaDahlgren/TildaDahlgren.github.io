Missing People

Project Management + SEM + Social Media Marketing

Missing People is a Swedish nonprofit organization that help families and the police to publish inquiries and arranging search operations of people gone missing. Today, Missing People is nationwide with more than 20 branches around the country and more than 500 000 followers on Facebook.

Brief: : "The challenge is to create more value for our Facebook followers, to introduce the followers to our other communication channels, to get more members and donors, without taking too much focus from the posts concerning missing people."


www.missingpeople.se

What


My role in this project was project manager and created the final product of a suggestive report on how Missing People could reach their goals, including ads.



How


Worked with Google Analytics, Google AdWords, Facebook Ad Manager and MailChimp.
