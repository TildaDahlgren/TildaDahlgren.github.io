About me

My name is Tilda and I am a Swedish creative technologist and digital designer. You can contact me at tildahlgren@gmail.com

Throughout my experience as a Digital Data Strategist student at Hyper Island in Stockholm, we combined the power of data analytics with design, optimization and result-driven marketing. At Hyper Island, one create solutions around real briefs made by real clients.

I believe in an agile, creative and empathic work environment. Where I can be curious and continuously learn new things as I strive for great design, great user experience and great technology.
